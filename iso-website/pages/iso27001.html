ISO 27001 - ISMS Overview

Clause 4 - Context of Organization

Organizations must identify internal and external factors affecting ISMS.

  • SWOT analysis
  • Stakeholder identification
  • Scope definition

Clause 6 - Risk Management

Risk assessment and treatment must be defined and documented.

  • Risk register
  • Risk treatment plan
  • Statement of Applicability
